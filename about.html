About Me!


Hi, I'm Ryan Stevenson, a Video Game and Software Engineer based in Plymouth. I am a student at Plymouth University studying Computing & Games Development. I'm also a Founder and Chairman of the UPSU Game Dev Society and a Founder and Developer of Blank Canvas Studio.

Interests

As a Game Developer I have a keen interest in gaming in both the mainstream scene and indie scene, I also take interest in technology both generally and in the games environment. Outisde of development I like to think I'm a sociable person and enjoy friend gatherings. I find interest in going to the gym and the occasional swim when I can fit them in. Naturally due to the nature of my development I am also an avid gamer, enjoying RPGs and TBS games as well as the occasional adventure and shooter!
